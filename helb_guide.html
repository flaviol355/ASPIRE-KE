Understanding HELB Loans

Higher Education Loans Board (HELB) loans are a lifeline for many Kenyan students pursuing higher education. Hereâ€™s a comprehensive guide to help you understand and apply for HELB loans:

1. Eligibility Criteria

HELB loans are available to undergraduate and postgraduate students enrolled in Kenyan institutions. Confirm your eligibility before applying.

2. Application Process

Visit the HELB portal and complete the application form. Ensure you provide accurate details and upload all required documents.

3. Loan Disbursement

HELB disburses loans in two tranches: one for tuition and the other for upkeep. Monitor your account for updates on disbursement.

4. Repayment Guidelines

Repayment begins one year after completing your studies. Keep track of your repayment schedule to avoid penalties.
